Social Algorithm Team at Tencent

We are a group of researchers and engineers, whose interests are to develop effective and efficient algorithms and models to optimize and analyze the experience of users in practical social networks. Our technologies have been deployed in various applications across dozens of products at Tencent Games, serving a massive number of active users on billion scales.
